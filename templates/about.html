about the project project.

The Project Project fosters collaboration between college innovators. We were founded at Harvard in July 2020 with the belief that students can complete increadible, self-directed projects with the proper team and support. We provide a space for similarly-minded people to connect and do exactly that. Here's how it works:
  • Apply:
    • Students at supported colleges (currently just Harvard) apply by describing their interests and previous projects. We are looking for people at all skill levels with an entrepreneurial mindset and a lot of passion. We are focused on computer science work (it lends itself to remote collaboration), but all disciplines are welcome.
  • Join:
    • Applicants who we think will fit in our community will recieve an invitation to join as members. They will be able to login to the collaborative space, access membership perks, and join our email list.
  • Collaborate:
    • Members can suggest project ideas that they have. These might be anything from a research paper to an app or website to a full-fledge startup. These suggestions are posted to the community-wide collaborative space where members can browse and join projects that pique their interest. Creators retain full control of their projects and who can join the team.
  • Get Support:
    • Completed projects will be publicized on our website. We will help developers get access to cloud computing resources or data they might need. We will help connect entrepreneurial projects to Venture Capital firms or other funding sources.
We hope you will apply! If you want to join, but your school or area of study is not supported, please reach out. We look forward to hearing from you!
